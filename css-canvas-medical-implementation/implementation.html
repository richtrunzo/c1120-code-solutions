1. Making the Decision to Switch

You should only move forward with Canvas if and when you have determined that
we far outperform other vendors and offer an unmatched return on investment. Our
goal is to give you as much information as possible to make this determination one
way or the other. We will provide access to a sandbox of the Canvas application for
you to get a feel for the platform as well as references to current customers to talk
about their experience.

2. Interfaces and Community Integration

Canvas is meant to integrate with your existing interfaces and community
resources. At a minimum, this means porting over your fax number, enrolling your
payers with our clearinghouse, and getting bidirectional lab feeds running. We'll
also load your preferred specialists and imaging centers into Canvas so that
sending referrals and closing loops is seamless. Finally, if interfaces with health
information exchanges or other information technology vendors are important, we
will work with you to determine what we can deliver and when.

3. Data Migration

The first thing we will do is gain access to a full backup of your current data, which
we have executed successfully with both remotely hosted and on-premise EMR
databases. Then our work begins to import into Canvas. Patient demographics and
information like medications, problems, and allergies is relatively straightforward.
While importing clinical notes and documents is more challenging, our team has
been able to do this at a high level of fidelity with a wide variety of EMRs.

4. Training and Site Visit

We will work with you to configure Canvas properly, including clinical content,
charge schedules, and much more. Role-based trainings will be done a few weeks
prior to go-live to familiarize relevant team members with clinical, practice
management, and billing workflows. Either before or during go-live, one of our
team can visit your practice to work side-by-side with you on implementation and
training.

5. Go-Live and Tuning

Go-live is when you start using Canvas with real patients in your practice. We will be
with you, either in-person or remotely depending on your preference, to make sure
everything goes smoothly. There is always fine tuning to do once you have a
handful of encounters in Canvas under your belt: changing clinical microtemplates,
setting up new automations, modifying practice management settings, and more.
We are here to help with it all.

6. Sustained Performance

All EMR contracts should contain assurances against broken promises and provide
support over the long term. Our Performance Warranty means that if we are not
meeting expectations, you can terminate the agreement and receive all payments
back. We do not expect this to happen). For our Standard plans, we also offer a
MIPS guarantee: if you receive a negative adjustment, we will cover it And for
everyone, we provide unlimited 24/7 support at no charge. We expect this to be a
long, productive partnership for us both.
